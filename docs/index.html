Delay-Day Hasselt 11 October 2019

A one-day symposium on delay equations was organized in Utrecht in February 2019 by Odo Diekmann, Stephan van Gils, Yuri Kuznetsov and Sjoerd Verduyn Lunel. Among the participants there was consensus that it would be a good idea to have an informal regional meeting about delay equations more regularly, say, twice a year.

The first such follow-up meeting will be held in Hasselt on Friday 11 October 2019. It is organized by Maikel Bosschaert (Hasselt), Sebastiaan Janssens (Utrecht) and Peter de Maesschalck (Hasselt).

Invited speakers

  • Odo Diekmann (Utrecht)
  • Hans-Otto Walther (Giessen)
  • Jan Sieber (Exeter)
  • Thomas Erneux (Brussels)
  • Babette de Wolff (Berlin)
